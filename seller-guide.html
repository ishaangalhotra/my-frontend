This guide will walk you through the complete process of adding products to your QuickLocal seller account and making them visible to customers on the marketplace.

Prerequisites

What you need before adding products

1

Seller Account

You must have a registered seller account on QuickLocal. If you don't have one, contact our support team.

2

Product Information

Gather all necessary product details including:

  • Product name and description
  • High-quality product images
  • Pricing information
  • Stock quantity
  • Product category
3

Technical Requirements

Ensure you have:

  • Stable internet connection
  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  • Product images in JPG, PNG, or WebP format

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to add your products

1

Access Seller Dashboard

Log in to your QuickLocal seller account and navigate to the Seller Dashboard.

Tip

Bookmark the seller dashboard URL for quick access: https://quicklocal.com/seller-dashboard

2

Navigate to Products Section

In the seller dashboard, click on the "Products" tab to access product management.

You'll see a list of your existing products and an "Add New Product" button.

3

Fill Product Information

Complete the product form with the following required information:

4

Upload Product Images

Upload high-quality images of your product:

  • Drag and drop images or click to browse
  • Upload up to 10 images per product
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
  • Recommended size: 800x600 pixels or larger
  • First image will be the main product image

Image Tips

Use clear, well-lit images that show your product from multiple angles. Avoid blurry or low-quality photos.

5

Set Product Status

Choose the publication status for your product:

  • Active - Product is visible to customers
  • Draft - Product is saved but not visible
  • Archived - Product is hidden from customers
6

Save and Publish

Click "Save Product" to create your product listing. If you set the status to "Active", it will be immediately visible on the marketplace.

API Reference

For developers: Direct API integration

Upload Product API

POST /api/seller/products

Request Headers:

Authorization: Bearer YOUR_JWT_TOKEN Content-Type: multipart/form-data

Request Body (Form Data):

name: "Product Name" description: "Product description" price: 99.99 stock: 50 category: "category_id" images: [file1, file2, ...] status: "active"

Response:

{ "success": true, "message": "Product uploaded successfully", "data": { "product": { "id": "product_id", "name": "Product Name", "price": 99.99, "stock": 50, "status": "active", "slug": "product-name-1234567890" } } }

Product Visibility

How products become visible to customers

Immediate Visibility

Products with Active status are immediately visible on:

  • Main marketplace page
  • Category pages
  • Search results
  • Product recommendations

Product Discovery

Customers can find your products through:

  • Search: Full-text search across product names and descriptions
  • Categories: Browse by product categories
  • Filters: Price range, rating, availability
  • Recommendations: AI-powered product suggestions

Product Page Features

Each product page includes:

  • Product images with zoom functionality
  • Detailed product description
  • Pricing and discount information
  • Stock availability
  • Customer reviews and ratings
  • Add to cart functionality
  • Related products

Best Practices

Tips for successful product listings

Image Quality

  • Use high-resolution images (minimum 800x600 pixels)
  • Ensure good lighting and clear focus
  • Show product from multiple angles
  • Include close-up shots of important features
  • Avoid watermarks or text overlays

Product Descriptions

  • Write clear, detailed descriptions
  • Include key features and specifications
  • Use relevant keywords for better search visibility
  • Mention any warranties or guarantees
  • Keep descriptions honest and accurate

Pricing Strategy

  • Research competitor prices
  • Set competitive but profitable prices
  • Consider offering discounts for bulk orders
  • Update prices regularly based on market conditions
  • Be transparent about any additional costs

Inventory Management

  • Keep stock levels updated
  • Set up low stock alerts
  • Plan for seasonal demand
  • Monitor product performance
  • Remove or archive out-of-stock items

Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions

Product Not Visible

Issue: Product is saved but not showing on the marketplace

Solution: Check that the product status is set to "Active"

Image Upload Failed

Issue: Images won't upload

Solution: Ensure images are in supported formats (JPG, PNG, WebP) and under 5MB each

Form Validation Errors

Issue: Cannot save product due to validation errors

Solution: Fill in all required fields marked with *

Slow Loading

Issue: Dashboard or product form loads slowly

Solution: Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page
