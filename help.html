Frequently Asked Questions

How fast is QuickLocal delivery?
QuickLocal offers ultra-fast 20-minute delivery in select areas. Standard delivery takes 1-2 hours depending on your location and order size.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and cash on delivery for orders under $50.
Can I cancel my order after placing it?
You can cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After that, please contact our support team for assistance.
How do I become a seller on QuickLocal?
To become a seller, register for a seller account, complete the verification process, and start adding your products. Our team will guide you through the process.
What if I receive a damaged item?
If you receive a damaged item, take photos and contact our support team within 24 hours. We'll arrange a replacement or refund.
